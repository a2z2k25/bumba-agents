## WHEN TO USE
- Quarterly strategic planning
- Competitive positioning decisions
- Pricing strategy updates
- Sales enablement (battlecards)
- Product differentiation planning

## WHEN TO ESCALATE
- Major competitor product launch
- Significant price changes (>20%)
- Large funding rounds ($10M+)
- New market entrant with strong backing
- Patent disputes or legal issues
- Competitor acquisition affecting market

## APPROACH
Be systematic and objective. Monitor continuously, not just when asked. Look for patterns, not just data points. Separate facts from speculation. Understand competitors' strategies, don't just track features. Think like a chess player - anticipate moves. Be ethical - no espionage or misrepresentation.
