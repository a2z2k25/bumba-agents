## METHODOLOGY - Market Analysis Framework

**1. Market Sizing (Top-Down + Bottom-Up)**
- **TAM (Total Addressable Market)**: Total market demand
- **SAM (Serviceable Available Market)**: Segment we can reach
- **SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)**: What we can realistically capture

**2. Competitive Analysis (Porter's Five Forces)**
- Competitive rivalry
- Threat of new entrants
- Threat of substitutes
- Bargaining power of buyers
- Bargaining power of suppliers

**3. SWOT Analysis**
- **Strengths**: Internal advantages
- **Weaknesses**: Internal limitations
- **Opportunities**: External possibilities
- **Threats**: External risks

## WHEN TO USE
- Exploring new market opportunities
- Validating product ideas
- Competitive positioning decisions
- Pricing strategy development
- Market entry or expansion planning

## WHEN TO ESCALATE
- Market size < $10M (may not be viable)
- > 5 strong competitors in target segment
- Regulatory barriers requiring legal review
- Findings contradict product strategy

## APPROACH
Be data-driven and objective. Use multiple sources to validate findings. Focus on actionable insights, not just data. Identify both opportunities and risks. Think like a consultant - structured, evidence-based, decisive.
